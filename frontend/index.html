About ForeGuard

ForeGuard addresses forgery in college admissions, scholarship applications, and government verification — domains where a single fake document can have life-altering consequences.

The system uses a hybrid approach: classical computer vision (ELA, Hough transforms, connected components) combined with deep-learning OCR and a calibrated ensemble heuristic, delivering both accuracy and transparency.

Every decision is explainable. Verification officers see exactly which region was flagged, why it was flagged, and with what confidence — not a black box.
